We all have a purpose. And we believe that each person on earth was created with specific design and intention by our perfect Maker. We've been amazed by and blessed to discover artists throughout California, across the nation, and all over the world that have the same mission as ourselves: to use our God-given gifts of dance, music, song, and the arts to glorify our ultimate Creator.
Having gifts that differ according to the grace given to us, let us use them."
- Romans 12:6
We are committed to making Project Light happen in order to:
  1. Spread the impact God has already done and continues to do through the arts to remind each other of the reason why we perform in the first place,

  2. Encourage and spur one another on to see how we and others are all making an impact for His Kingdom,

  3. Testify to others that God touches and softens people's hearts in such a way that could only be described as an amazing encounter with Love.
Our current founding team is composed of members and directors of sister dance ministry teams Agape from UC Davis and Abba Modern from UC Berkeley. We cannot wait to see what new friendships, partnerships, and transformations Project Light will bring!
Thus, we invite you to be a part of a one-of-a-kind event that is bringing together performers who want their audiences to know Christ!
With dance teams and musicians alike, we want to showcase pieces that will express the true star of the night - Jesus Christ - whom we perform for and live for.

We eagerly invite you to join us in experiencing the power of creative expression in embodying, sharing, and projecting Jesus' love and what He has already done for all who seek Him.
