We all have a purpose. And we believe that each person on earth was created with specific design and intention by our perfect Maker. We've been amazed by and blessed to discover artists throughout California, across the nation, and all over the world that have the same mission as ourselves: to use our God-given gifts of dance, music, song, and the arts to glorify our ultimate Creator.
Having gifts that differ according to the grace given to us, let us use them." - Romans 12:6
We want to display the impact God has already done through the arts to remind each other of the reason why we perform in the first place, to encourage and spur one another on to see how we and others are all making an impact for His Kingdom, and to display to others that God touches and softens people's hearts in such a way that could only be described as an amazing encounter with love.
Our current founding team is composed of members and directors of sister dance ministry teams Agape from UC Davis and Abba Modern from UC Berkeley. We cannot wait to see what new friendships and relationships Project Light can bring together. Our event date is scheduled to be in San Francisco on April 30th, 2016.
Thus, we want to invite you to be a part of an event that is bringing together performers who want their audiences to know Christ! Our performer's application will be found here. If you have any questions or concerns, the best way to reach me will be either via email or our Facebook page.
With dance teams and musicians alike, we want to showcase pieces that will express the true star of the night - Jesus Christ - whom we perform for and live for.

Join us in experiencing the power of creative expression in embodying and sharing Jesus' love and what He has already done for all who seek Him.
