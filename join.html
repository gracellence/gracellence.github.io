How you can help us make Project Light a reality!

PERFORM

As a performer, you'll get to participate in a special fellowship time before the showcase during which all the performers will get to share and learn about how God has been moving in each other's work, complimentary food, and the priceless experience of being part of a unique showcase in unity with other Christian artists!

Apply to perform here.

VOLUNTEER

We're always grateful to have more helping hands in any aspect of operations: media, finance, marketing, communications, fundraising, sponsorships, logistics, admin, event planning, etc.

As part of the official Project Light coordinating team, you'll get an extraordinary experience of being a part of making this event happen. You'll get to learn and strengthen valuable skills to add to your own professional development and yes, your resume.

Not to mention, you'll also get to befriend and work with an awesome team of Christ-honoring, logistics-loving people who share a vision and passion of bringing this event to life and sharing God's truth and love with the world!

Fill out the volunteer survey here.

DONATE

Although our Indiegogo campaign has ended, we are still looking for funding and financial contributions to pay for the costs of this event.

100% of funds go into making this showcase happen, including costs for:
the venue rental, crew, and insurance ($12,850); ticketing services ($450), publicity materials and hospitality materials for performers ($800), honorariums and plane tickets for featured artists ($6,000), and more. Our estimated total cost is $20,100.

In addition, all profits will be donated to New Day for Children, a faith-based non-profit organization that seeks to recover children caught in the despair of child sex trafficking and to restore their stolen childhoods.

OTHER WAYS YOU CAN HELP

  1. Please pray for us, for the showcase, and for all artists and creators around the world to use their God-given talents for God Himself!
  2. Please share about Project Light with your friends -- we need all the help we can get in spreading the word and raising enough funds to make this event happen.
  3. Lastly, please follow/"like" us on social media, and share them/invite your friends to follow us, too! Again, we are relying heavily on word-of-mouth to get all the support that we can, as well as to sell tickets for the showcase itself!
